    📚Resource Guide
    The most current version is always available under Resource Documents. Refer to it first for any internal questions. If you have suggestions for additions, please contact Marjanae.
    🖨Printers
    All equipment is separated by revenue source. You must use your unit’s printer or the shared printers in Division Operations (supply room, behind the front desk, or bypass through the window). Always ensure your printer settings are set to Black & White — even on a B&W printer, a color setting results in color charges. Contact your supervisor before printing anything beyond letterhead in color.
    📍EIO (Employee In/Out)
    EIO is essential for supervisor time card management, employee safety, emergency response, and locating staff when needed. Colleagues also rely on it to determine availability for collaboration and communication. EIO is a tool for efficiency, safety, and accessibility — not surveillance. Using it correctly fosters an organized, secure, and responsive work environment for everyone.
